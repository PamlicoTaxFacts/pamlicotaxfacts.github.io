# Pamlico Tax Facts

Independent, sourced analysis of Pamlico County, North Carolina's FY2026-27 budget and the 2026 property revaluation.

**Live site:** https://pamlicotaxfacts.github.io

## What this is
A single-page, interactive explainer of the county's proposed property-tax rate after the 2026 revaluation — including the gap between the proposed rate (52.5¢) and the published revenue-neutral rate (43.5¢), where the money goes, and how the county compares to peers. All figures are drawn from official county budget documents, commissioner minutes, NCDOR tax-rate tables, NC General Statutes, and local reporting; sources are listed at the bottom of the page.

## Corrections
This page is meant to stay current as the FY2026-27 budget is finalized. Spotted an error or have a documented update? Open an issue.
