Show up

Budget meetings & how to speak

Commissioners decide in open meetings, and the FY2026-27 budget must be adopted by June 30. Public pressure works best in the room.

Regular meetings
1st & 3rd Monday, 7:00 PM
Patsy H. Sadler Room, 2nd floor, Pamlico County Courthouse, Bayboro
Budget public hearing
Date TBA — watch the agenda
Required before adoption; usually set at a June meeting
How to speak:
  • Public comment is typically signed up for with the Clerk before the meeting — call (252) 745-3133 to confirm the sign-up and time limit.
  • Keep it to ~2-3 minutes: state your name/township, the ask (adopt revenue-neutral 43.5¢), and one personal fact (your valuation jump or bill increase).
  • Can't attend? Email all commissioners (above) — written comments are part of the record.

Take formal action

File paperwork with the county

Two filings that actually move the needle:

1 · Appeal your property value. Every successful appeal shrinks the inflated tax base the windfall is built on. If your 2026 value jumped, appeal it through the Board of Equalization & Review.

County Tax Office & appeal info →

2 · Request public records. Anyone can ask for budget documents, the revenue-neutral calculation, appeal counts, and more (NC G.S. §132). One click drafts a records request to the Clerk — edit and send.

A full set of ready-to-send request templates (budget, appeals, spending) is part of this project — ask via the form above and we'll send them.
